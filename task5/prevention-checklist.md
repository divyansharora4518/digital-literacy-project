# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips:
1. Always use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts.
3. Do not click on unknown links or suspicious emails/messages.
4. Avoid sharing personal information on social media.
5. Always verify the identity of unknown callers or messages.
6. Keep your devices updated with latest security updates.

## UPI & Financial Safety:
7. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.
8. Double-check UPI IDs and payment details before sending money.
9. Avoid scanning unknown QR codes received via WhatsApp or SMS.
10. Use only official banking or payment apps.
