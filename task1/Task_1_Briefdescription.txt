                                           Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
For this task, I used Canva to create a digital literacy awareness infographic. Canva is an easy-to-use online design tool that provides various templates, icons, and design elements, which helped me create an attractive and informative visual.
My infographic explains the concept of digital literacy and its importance for students. It covers topics such as what digital literacy means, safe internet practices, and useful digital tools for students. I included points about using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and using tools like Google Docs and Canva for academic work. The design uses simple icons and a clean layout to make the information easy to understand.
One challenge I faced was selecting the right combination of colors and arranging the content in a way that looks visually balanced. However, I found it interesting to experiment with different templates and icons, which made the final design more engaging and effective.
